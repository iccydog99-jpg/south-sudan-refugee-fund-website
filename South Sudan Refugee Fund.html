Our Mission

The South Sudan Refugee Fund is dedicated to providing emergency relief, shelter, clean water, and educational opportunities to South Sudanese refugees and internally displaced people. We work with local partners to deliver sustainable, community-based solutions that foster resilience and hope.

The Crisis

Years of conflict, violence, and climate-related disasters have led to one of the largest refugee crises in Africa. More than 2.3 million South Sudanese people are refugees in neighboring countries, with millions more internally displaced. They face severe food insecurity, lack of clean water, and limited access to healthcare and education.

The situation is dire, but with your help, we can make a difference.
